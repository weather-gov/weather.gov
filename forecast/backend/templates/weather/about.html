NWS mission

Provide weather, water and climate data, forecasts, warnings, and impact-based decision support services for the protection of life and property and enhancement of the national economy.

This website

The National Weather Service (NWS) provides science-based forecasts and warnings to protect life and property. It makes sure important weather information reaches the broadest possible audience.

This website provides information to empower everyone to protect themselves and their communities.
